About Me

Hello! I'm Hershey Agustin, a web designer and developer based in Edmonton, Alberta. I have a deep appreciation for connecting with others and understanding their needs, which drives my passion for creating designs that really connect. One of my strengths is connecting with people's emotions and imaginations, allowing me to empathize and create designs that speak to them. This ability comes from my varied experiences, including working in industries such as tourism, retail, and marketing. These roles taught me the importance of understanding people from various walks of life with unique needs. Over time, I was drawn to web design because I enjoy building websites and staying updated with evolving technologies and trends.

My design approach focuses on creating simple, readable, and accessible websites. I use fonts, colors, and contrasts to make designs visually appealing and user-friendly while always keeping navigation and interaction in mind. I’ve worked on various projects to strengthen my web design and development skills. In my Web Design and Usability course, I created an accessible design that focused on color schemes, typography, and branding, earning an A+ for exceeding course goals. I’ve also built a fully functional WordPress website, customizing themes, plugins, and widgets for a dynamic user experience. Additionally, for an SEO-optimized project, I developed a site that ranked higher and improved performance by testing responsiveness and using Google Analytics to refine design and functionality.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and learning about different cultures. These experiences give me fresh perspectives and inspire my designs. I believe that understanding diverse cultures helps me create websites that connect with a wider audience.

I create websites that engage your audience and support your business goals. I’m always open to collaborating and exploring creative opportunities. If you'd like to discuss design or website building, feel free to reach out. Let’s connect!
