About Me

Hello! I'm Hershey Agustin, a web designer and developer based in Edmonton, Alberta. I have a deep appreciation for connecting with others and understanding their needs, which drives my passion for creating designs that really connect. One of my strengths is connecting with people's emotions and imaginations, allowing me to empathize and create designs that speak to them. This ability comes from my varied experiences, including working in industries such as tourism, retail, and marketing. These roles taught me the importance of understanding people from various walks of life with unique needs. Over time, I was drawn to web design because I enjoy building websites and staying updated with evolving technologies and trends.

My design approach focuses on creating simple, readable, and accessible websites. I use fonts, colors, and contrasts to make designs visually appealing and user-friendly while always keeping navigation and interaction in mind. I’ve worked on various projects to strengthen my web design and development skills. In my Web Design and Usability course, I created an accessible design that focused on color schemes, typography, and branding, earning an A+ for exceeding course goals. I’ve also built a fully functional WordPress website, customizing themes, plugins, and widgets for a dynamic user experience. Additionally, for an SEO-optimized project, I developed a site that ranked higher and improved performance by testing responsiveness and using Google Analytics to refine design and functionality.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and learning about different cultures. These experiences give me fresh perspectives and inspire my designs. I believe that understanding diverse cultures helps me create websites that connect with a wider audience.

I create websites that engage your audience and support your business goals. I’m always open to collaborating and exploring creative opportunities. If you'd like to discuss design or website building, feel free to reach out. Let’s connect!

FitnessTime

Description: In this project, I developed a Fitness Time Website for a local gym, focusing on creating a simple, user-friendly solution for managing services, trainers, partners, and plans. The goal was to make it easy for non-technical gym staff to update content. I used WordPress, leveraging posts, categories, and tags to organize and manage the site’s content effectively. This project showcased my skills in WordPress theme development, child themes, and site hierarchy while creating a system that requires no advanced technical knowledge for the gym staff.

Role:

  • WordPress Developer : I created the website using WordPress, setting up a child theme to make it easy to maintain and update. I focused on making sure the staff could manage content easily using WordPress posts, categories, and tags.
  • UI/UX Designer and Web Developer : I designed a simple, user-friendly website that works on all devices and focused on creating an easy and engaging user experience.

Skills Used:

  • WordPress (Hierarchy & Child Themes) : I set up the website using a child theme, which allowed for easy customization while keeping everything organized.
  • WordPress Posts, Categories & Tags : I used these features to make it simple for the gym staff to organize and update their content, like services and trainers.
  • CSS : I used CSS to apply custom styles to the website.

Challenges & Process: The biggest challenge was making sure the website was easy for the gym staff to use since they had no technical experience. To solve this, I made sure everything was manageable through the WordPress dashboard, with posts and categories that the staff could update without needing to deal with any code.I worked closely with the instructor to understand the project requirements and how the site should function. After that, I built the website, organized the content, and ensured it was easy to update and maintain.

Outcomes: This project helped me improve my WordPress skills, especially in working with themes and content management. It also taught me how to design a website that is easy for non-technical users to manage. The gym now has a website that they can easily update, and it provides a good experience for both the staff and their clients.
